Ah, "teeming"! Let's explore the concept of abundance and overflowing with this word!

Usage: When something is "teeming," it's like an anthill buzzing with activity—it means there is a great quantity or multitude of things, people, or creatures, creating a lively and bustling atmosphere.

Etymology: The word "teeming" comes from the Old English word "tēman," which means "to produce offspring" or "to bring forth." It's like describing a situation where there is an overflowing abundance or profusion.
