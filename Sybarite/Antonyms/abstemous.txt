Ah, "abstemious"! Let's explore the concept of moderate and restrained behavior with this word!

Usage: When someone is described as "abstemious," it's like they have a knack for balance and self-discipline—it refers to a person who practices moderation in their consumption of food, drink, or other indulgences.

Etymology: The word "abstemious" comes from the Latin word "abstēmius," which means "restrained" or "temperate." It's like describing someone who exercises self-control and avoids excesses in their lifestyle.
